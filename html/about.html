WHAT IS LENARS'

Learners' website is built on behalf of all the science students of a well known prestigious school in IJEBU DISTRICT by name IJEBU MUSLIM COLLEGE.It is a free website for all students willing and ready to learn.

WHY WAS LENARS' BUILT?

The reason for building this website was inspired by one of the major problems facing the learners,teachers and the whole educational system at large which is the lack of necessary materials which the learners need for effective learning to take place.

HOW DOES LENARS' HELP TO IMROVE THE STANDARD OF EDUCATION

Learners' helps by providing basic educational needs such as textbooks,novels,dictionary for students
